Windows Painting by Pablo Bill Gates

I use oil paints to disrupt the purity of the blank canvas with abstract shapes and lines. I feature very loosely interpreted seascapes and sunsets prominently. I've lived my whole life steps away from the ocean, and I find it the most meaningful inspiration. My pieces attempt to join the joy of abandon with colour to create bold, bright, daring pieces.
